For five years, Stephanie Macias has been reading and considering submissions to American Short Fiction, and she’s been instrumental in running our in-person events in Austin. A virtual Swiss Army Knife of artistic pursuits (she’s a writer, photographer, visual artist, musician, and editor),

Stephanie is now set to bring her broad experience and enthusiasm for stories to a new role at American Short Fiction: that of managing editor. As the outgoing managing editor, I’m both grateful for and proud of the work I’ve gotten to do during my tenure, and I’m excited about what the future of the magazine looks like with Stephanie’s hand firmly on the wheel.
